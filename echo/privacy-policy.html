ECHO TERMINAL | PRIVACY POLICY

> Last updated: November 9, 2025
> PenguinWorks

1. Introduction

> Echo allows users to send and receive anonymous quotes.
> You do not need to create an account, and we do not collect personal information such as your name or email.
> Each session is authenticated anonymously via Firebase.

2. Information We Collect

> - Anonymous Firebase ID (auto-generated, no personal data)

> - Device information (non-identifiable)

> - Usage events (e.g., quote sent/received timestamps)

> No data is linked to your identity.

3. How Your Data Is Used

> - To enable sending and receiving quotes anonymously

> - To manage subscriptions and usage limits

> - To improve stability and user experience

> We never sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Subscriptions

> - Orbital-24 (Free): Send and receive every 24 hours

> - Nebula-12 (Paid): Send and receive every 12 hours

> Purchases are processed securely through your device's app store.

5. Data Storage and Security

> Data is stored securely on Firebase servers. Uninstalling the app deletes your local session and creates a new anonymous ID upon reinstall.

6. Third-Party Services

> Echo uses Firebase and RevenueCat to handle authentication and subscription management.
> These services follow their own privacy policies.

> - Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

> - RevenueCat: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

7. Your Rights

> Since no personal data is stored, there is no account to delete. However, you can reset your anonymous session by reinstalling the app.
